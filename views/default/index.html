Welcome!

Did your batch upload get stuck or partially failed?

Do you want to know which photos made it and which did not?

This app is for you!

SetInspektor will compare the names of the pictures you uploaded in a certain set, against those in a folder of your computer and tell you which ones are missing from the set.

Create a new set on flickr and add in all the photos you want to check, then click this button to authorize setinspektor to access your flickr account.
